The Situation

Something is quietly
shifting here.

"The social warmth is real. The weekend markets, the bilingual coffee hours, the genuine friendships across language lines. But underneath that warmth, two separate economic realities are hardening."

But they increasingly do not share economic opportunity, business infrastructure, or the kind of cross-cultural collaboration that builds lasting shared prosperity.
